What We Offer

Medical Waste Collection

Fully compliant removal of infectious, pathological, sharps & pharmaceutical waste, up to Schedule 5.

Sanitary Bin Service

Discrete, hygienic servicing and disposal of feminine hygiene units in restaurants, offices, shops.

Bin & Container Supply

SABS-approved containers, sharps boxes, liners — correctly marked and supplied for your needs.

Documentation & TrainingWe supply manifests, certificates of destruction, route logs, and staff guidance on safe segregation.

How It Works

1. Assess

We visit your site or review via photos to estimate waste volumes & infrastructure needs.

2. Provision

We provide liners, bins, sharps boxes and train your staff on colour coding and labelling.

3. Collect

Scheduled collection by certified staff with full chain-of-custody manifest.

4. Dispose & Certify

Waste is processed at approved facilities and a certificate of destruction is issued.
